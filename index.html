Siblings and Family Friends

21st August 2024

A wild and wonderful variety night hosted by character comedians and IRL sisters Maddy and Marina Bye of critically acclaimed Siblings, starring their family friends (who happen to be the best comedians at the Fringe).

Expect stand-up, clowning, sketch-comedy, drag, dance and more in these three exclusive nights.

Winner: Brighton Fringe Best Show Award 2021. Nominee: Comedian's Choice Award 2022, British Comedy Guide.
